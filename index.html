College Information Chatbot

AI Based Student Support System

Project Description

The College Information Chatbot is an AI powered system developed using Dialogflow. It helps students quickly obtain information about the college such as admission process, courses offered, fee structure, hostel facilities, placement details and contact information. The chatbot automates student queries and provides instant responses without human intervention.

Objectives

  • Provide instant information about college
  • Reduce workload of admission office
  • Help students get information 24/7
  • Improve communication between college and students
  • Automate frequently asked questions

Intents Used in Chatbot

  • Greeting
  • Admission Process
  • Eligibility Criteria
  • Courses Information
  • Fees Structure
  • Required Documents
  • Hostel Information
  • Placement Information
  • College Location
  • Contact Information
  • Default Welcome Intent
  • Default Fallback Intent
